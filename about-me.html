About Me

Hi, I’m Shivkumar Chaurasiya, currently pursuing my MBBS at Government Medical College. My journey into medicine began with successfully clearing the NEET entrance exam, and since then, I’ve been fully committed to becoming a skilled and compassionate doctor.

I have a keen interest in both the theoretical and practical aspects of medicine. Alongside my academic journey, I’ve developed essential medical skills such as basic patient assessment, clinical observation, case note writing, and an understanding of medical terminology. These are the building blocks that fuel my passion for learning and helping others.

In addition to my medical education, I’ve honed several soft and technical skills that support my growth. I’m known for my strong communication and interpersonal abilities, along with my leadership and task management skills. I’m also certified in MS Office, which I regularly use for organizing data, creating presentations, and managing reports.

Outside of academics, I’m someone who enjoys a balanced life. I’m an avid gamer, which helps me stay sharp and focused, and I love trekking, which keeps me grounded and connected to nature. These interests reflect my belief in maintaining a healthy mind and body.
